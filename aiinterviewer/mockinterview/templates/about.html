📌What is MockMate?

MockMate is a smart mock interview simulator designed to mimic real-world technical interviews. Using voice and text inputs, it quizzes you with topic-based questions, offers instant scoring, feedback, and a complete performance review. Practice and grow with your virtual interviewer.

👥Who is it for?

  • 🎓 Students prepping for tech and campus interviews
  • 🧑‍💼 Job seekers sharpening communication skills
  • 💻 Developers reviewing concepts before interviews
  • 🎤 Anyone who wants to build confidence in answering out loud

🚀How to Use MockMate?

  1. 🔍 Choose a topic: Python, Django, JavaScript, or React
  2. 🎧 Listen to or read each interview question
  3. 🎙️ Respond using voice or typed input
  4. ✅ Receive real-time feedback and scoring
  5. 📊 View your complete performance summary at the end
